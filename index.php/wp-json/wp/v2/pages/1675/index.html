GDPR and Data Retention Policy for PURE AGILITY LTD<\/p>\n

Purpose<\/strong><\/h3>\n

\u25cf The purpose of this policy is to outline PURE AGILITY LTD’s approach to
\nmanaging and retaining personal data in compliance with the General Data
\nProtection Regulation (GDPR) regarding data retention practices.
\nScope
\n\u25cf This policy applies to all departments and individuals responsible for data
\nretention at PURE AGILITY LTD and covers all types of data. It also applies to all geographic areas where PURE AGILITY LTD operates.<\/p>\n

Data Retention Principles<\/h4>\n

\u25cf 1. Lawful Basis for Retention: PURE AGILITY LTD will only retain personal data if there is a lawful basis for doing so.
\n\u25cf 2. Data Minimization: PURE AGILITY LTD will only retain the minimum amount
\nof personal data necessary to achieve the purpose for which it was collected.
\n\u25cf 3. Data Accuracy: PURE AGILITY LTD will take reasonable steps to ensure that personal data is accurate and up-to-date.<\/p>\n

\nRetention Periods<\/h4>\n

\u25cf 1. PURE AGILITY LTD will retain personal data only for as long as necessary to
\nfulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including legal requirements and business needs.
\n\u25cf 2. PURE AGILITY LTD will review and update its retention periods on a regular basis to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations.<\/p>\n

\nData Disposal<\/h4>\n

\u25cf 1. PURE AGILITY LTD will securely dispose of personal data once the retention period expires, through data deletion, anonymization, or archival processes.
\n\u25cf 2. PURE AGILITY LTD will ensure that all personal data is disposed of in a manner that is compliant with applicable laws and regulations.<\/p>\n

\nData Subject Rights<\/h4>\n

\u25cf 1. PURE AGILITY LTD recognizes the rights of data subjects under GDPR,
\nincluding the right to access, rectify, and erase their personal data.
\n\u25cf 2. PURE AGILITY LTD will respond to data subject requests in a timely and mannerly fashion within the context of data retention.<\/p>\n

\nData Security Measures<\/h4>\n

\u25cf 1. PURE AGILITY LTD will implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect retained data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
\n\u25cf 2. PURE AGILITY LTD will monitor and review its data security measures
\nregularly and update them as necessary to ensure ongoing compliance and
\neffectiveness.<\/p>\n

\nCompliance and Governance<\/h4>\n

\u25cf 1. PURE AGILITY LTD will establish mechanisms for monitoring and ensuring compliance with this policy.
\n\u25cf 2. PURE AGILITY LTD will assign responsibilities for data retention and
\ndisposal, including the appointment of a Data Protection Officer (DPO) where required.
\n\u25cf 3. PURE AGILITY LTD will conduct regular audits or reviews of its data retention practices to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations.<\/p>\n

\nThis GDPR and Data Retention Policy serves as a guiding document for PURE AGILITY LTD to establish consistent and compliant data retention practices while safeguarding the privacy rights of individuals.<\/h5>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
